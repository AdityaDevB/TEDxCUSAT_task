About TEDx

More Than
A Talk.

TEDx events are independently organized, bringing the spirit of TED to local communities. TEDx CUSAT is an event that gathers extraordinary minds from science, art, technology, and social change — all under one roof at Cochin University.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx events are fully planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community basis. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.
